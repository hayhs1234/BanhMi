Banh Mi VietNam - Great Tradition Food

Banh Mi is a famous Vietnamese dish, this dish is loved by many people around the world.

Introducing the dish

Banh Mi is a traditional Vietnamese food, often eaten in the morning.

Ingredients to prepare

  • Banh mi
  • Cold cuts, ham or pate
  • Cucumber, pickles, coriander
  • Chili sauce, soy sauce, mayonnaise or butter
